About Max Pipes & Fittings INC.

Max Pipes and Fittings Inc. is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality Buttweld Pipe Fittings. We supply stainless steel pipe fittings, inconel pipe fittings, monel pipe fittings, alloy steel pipe fittings, and hastelloy pipe fittings in different types of grades and sizes. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry. Our extensive range of products caters to diverse industrial applications, and we strive to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
